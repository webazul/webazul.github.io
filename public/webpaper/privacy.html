Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Summary: WebPaper processes your documents on your device. We collect only the minimum data needed to provide the service. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your documents belong to you.

1. Data Controller

WebPaper is a mobile application developed and operated by WebAzul, based in Portugal. This policy applies to the WebPaper app available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

Data Protection Contact: contacto@webazul.pt

2. What We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (required for authentication)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Language and locale preferences
  • Last login timestamp

2.2 Documents, Scans, and Files

Important: How your documents are stored depends on your plan. Please read this section carefully.

Aspect Free Plan (Local Storage) Premium Plan (Cloud Storage)
Where files are stored Only on your device's local filesystem On your device + Google Cloud Storage (encrypted backup)
Who has access Only you, on that specific device Only you, from any device logged into your account
What happens if you uninstall All documents are permanently lost — there is no backup Documents remain safely in the cloud and can be restored on any device
What happens if you change device Documents cannot be transferred Documents sync automatically to the new device
Our access to your files We have zero access — files never leave your device Files are encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not access, read, analyze, or share your document contents

WebPaper does not perform any automated analysis, OCR text extraction, machine learning training, or content scanning on your documents. Your files are stored exactly as you created them.

2.3 Signatures and Initials

Digital signature and initials images that you create are stored:

  • Free users: On your device only
  • Premium users: On your device and in cloud storage (for sync)

Signatures are used solely for the purpose of overlaying on documents you choose to sign. We do not use signatures for identity verification or any other purpose.

2.4 Purchase and Subscription Information

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We receive:

  • A purchase confirmation and transaction identifier
  • Subscription status (active, expired, grace period)
  • Product identifier and purchase platform

We never receive, process, or store your payment card details, billing address, or financial information. All payment processing is handled by Apple or Google under their respective terms.

2.5 Device Information

  • Push notification token (only if you enable notifications)
  • Device language and locale settings
  • Operating system and app version (for compatibility)

We do not collect device identifiers (UDID, IMEI), location data, contacts, calendar, or any data from other apps on your device.

2.6 Advertising Data (Free Users Only)

For free users, we display advertisements via Google AdMob. In this context, Google may collect:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
  • General device information for ad targeting
  • Ad interaction data

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking; Android: Settings > Google > Ads). Premium subscribers do not see any advertisements and no advertising data is collected.

See Google's Privacy Policy for details on Google's data practices.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide document scanning, organization, signing, and export services
  • To store and sync documents across devices (Premium users only)
  • To process and validate subscription purchases
  • To send push notifications you have opted into
  • To display advertisements (Free users only)
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To maintain and improve app stability and performance

We do not use your data for profiling, automated decision-making, or selling to third parties.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR — EEA/UK Users)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the United Kingdom, we process personal data based on:

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)): Account creation, document storage, subscription management
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): Push notifications, personalized advertising, optional analytics
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)): App security, fraud prevention, service improvement, debugging
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): Tax and accounting requirements for purchase records

You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal.

5. Data Storage, Security, and Encryption

  • Authentication: Handled by Firebase Authentication (Google), using industry-standard protocols
  • Cloud storage: Google Cloud Platform (GCP) with encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+)
  • Database: Cloud Firestore with per-user security rules — users can only access their own data
  • Local storage: Documents stored in the app's sandboxed filesystem, protected by the operating system
  • Access control: Server-side validation ensures no user can access another user's documents or data

While we implement commercially reasonable security measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords for your account.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on Google Cloud servers located outside your country of residence, including in the United States and European Union. These transfers are protected by:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Google's compliance certifications (ISO 27001, SOC 2, SOC 3)
  • The EU-US Data Privacy Framework, where applicable

8. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Cloud documents (Premium): Retained until you delete them or delete your account. Upon subscription expiry, documents remain accessible but no new uploads are allowed.
  • Local documents (Free): Remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app. We have no copy.
  • Purchase records: Retained for up to 7 years for legal and accounting obligations.
  • Debug logs: Stored on-device only, maximum 200 entries, never transmitted to our servers unless you manually export them.
